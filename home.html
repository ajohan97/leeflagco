ABOUT US

Lee Flag Co. is a brand owned by Lee Products Ltd which launched in 2021 to bring unique, high-quality designs at an affordable price to the everyday man. Our journey began on Facebook Marketplace with a vision to redefine the flag industry by offering a diverse range of meticulously crafted flags that resonate with individual stories and collective experiences.

Each flag is made from 100% Polyester and double stitched along the edges to prevent fraying and give each flag a longer life. By focusing on affordability without compromising on quality, we aim to bring meaningful flags to everyone.

At Lee Flag Co., we stand behind the quality of our products with unwavering confidence. Each flag you purchase from us is backed by a one-year guarantee, a testament to our commitment to durability and customer satisfaction. If you encounter any issues or defects within the first year of purchase, we will gladly replace your flag, just send us an email at quality@leeflagco.com.
